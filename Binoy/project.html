Kannur Villa
Residence

Residential 2023 Kannur, Kerala

Project Overview

A Home That Belongs
to Its Landscape

The Kannur Villa Residence is a 3,200 sq.ft. contemporary tropical home designed to harmonize with the lush green landscape of northern Kerala. Set on a sloping site with mature mango and jackfruit trees, the design preserves every significant tree while creating a fluid relationship between indoor and outdoor living.

The architecture draws from Kerala's vernacular tradition — sloped roofs, open courtyards, and deep verandas — reinterpreted through a contemporary lens. Natural ventilation and passive cooling strategies reduce dependence on mechanical systems, while carefully placed openings frame views of the surrounding canopy.

Location Kannur, Kerala, India
Year 2022 – 2023
Area 3,200 sq.ft.
Typology Single Family Residence
Structure RCC Frame with Laterite Infill
Status Completed
