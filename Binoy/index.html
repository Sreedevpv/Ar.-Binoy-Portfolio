01 · About

Architecture Is the
Thoughtful Making of Space

With over eight years of practice rooted in the landscapes of Kerala, I approach architecture as a dialogue between the built environment and the natural world. Every project begins with listening — to the site, the climate, and the people who will inhabit the space.

My work spans residential, commercial, and institutional projects, each driven by a commitment to sustainability, material honesty, and spatial clarity. From vernacular reinterpretations to contemporary tropical designs, I seek to create structures that are both of their place and timeless.

"A building should appear to grow easily from its site and be shaped to harmonize with its surroundings."
